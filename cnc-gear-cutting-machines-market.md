<p><p>CNC gear cutting machines are classified into four main types based on the method of gear cutting they employ - Gear Hobbing Machine, Gear Shaping Machine, Gear Shaving Machine, and Gear Grinding Machine. Gear hobbing machines use a cutting tool called a hob to create gears, while gear shaping machines use a cutting tool that reciprocates in a vertical motion. Gear shaving machines use a shaving cutter to improve gear accuracy and surface finish, and gear grinding machines utilize abrasive wheels to achieve precise gear shapes and sizes.</p></p>
