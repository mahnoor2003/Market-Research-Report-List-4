<p><p>A foot valve is a type of check valve designed to be placed at the bottom of a pump suction line in order to prevent the pump from losing its prime when it is turned off. These valves are typically used in applications where there is a possibility of the pump losing its prime due to air entering the suction line. Foot valves are essential in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of pumping systems by preventing backflow and maintaining a consistent flow of fluids.</p><p>The foot valves market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
