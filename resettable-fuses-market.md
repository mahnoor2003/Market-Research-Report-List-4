<p><p>Resettable fuses are available in two main types - polymer and ceramic. Polymer resettable fuses are made of conductive polymers and are more commonly used due to their low cost and high flexibility. They are suitable for lower voltage applications. On the other hand, ceramic resettable fuses are made of ceramic materials and are preferred for high voltage applications as they provide better protection. Both types of resettable fuses are widely used in electronic devices for overcurrent protection.</p></p>
