<p><p>Organ transplantation market includes different types such as Autograft, Allograft, and others. Autograft involves the transplant of tissue from one part of the body to another in the same individual. Allograft refers to the transplantation of tissue or organs from a donor to a recipient of the same species. Other types of transplants may include xenografts (from a different species) or synthetic implants. Each type of transplant has its own benefits and risks, depending on the specific medical condition and patient's needs.</p></p>
