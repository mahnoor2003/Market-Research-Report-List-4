<p><strong>The Powder Polyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydroxyl Terminated</li><li>Carboxyl Terminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder polyester resins can be classified into two main types based on their chemical structure and functional groups: hydroxyl terminated and carboxyl terminated resins. Hydroxyl terminated resins have hydroxyl (-OH) groups at their ends, making them suitable for curing with isocyanates or blocked isocyanates. On the other hand, carboxyl terminated resins have carboxyl (-COOH) groups, allowing them to cure with epoxy resins or using a self-crosslinking mechanism. These different types cater to various applications in the powder coatings industry.</p></p>
