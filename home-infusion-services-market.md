Subcutaneously involves administering medications under the skin, usually with the help of a small needle. The "Others" market consists of other methods of delivering medications at home, such as intramuscular injections. These services are convenient for patients who require long-term treatment or frequent medication administration in the comfort of their own homes.</p></p>
