<p><strong>The Plastic Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Plastic Film Capacitors</li><li>Radial Plastic Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic film capacitors are categorized into two main types, axial and radial. Axial plastic film capacitors have leads extending from each end and are ideal for through-hole mounting on PCBs. Radial plastic film capacitors, on the other hand, have leads emerging from the same end and are commonly used in surface mounting applications. Both types offer high reliability, low losses, and excellent stability, making them popular choices for a wide range of electronic applications.</p></p>
