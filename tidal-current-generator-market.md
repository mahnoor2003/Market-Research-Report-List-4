<p><p>Tidal current generators can be categorized into two main market types based on their power generation orientation: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal power generation systems are typically placed on the sea floor to harness the kinetic energy of tidal currents in a horizontal orientation. On the other hand, vertical power generation systems are installed vertically in the water column to efficiently capture the energy of moving tides. Both market types seek to maximize power output from tidal currents for sustainable energy generation.</p></p>
