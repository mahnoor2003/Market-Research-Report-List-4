<p><p>Arc welding equipment market consists of three main types of equipment: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual equipment requires the welder to control the welding process entirely. Semi-automatic equipment provides some assistance to the welder, such as controlling wire feed speed. Fully automatic equipment carries out the welding process independently without the need for direct human control. Each type of equipment offers varying levels of efficiency, ease of use, and precision to cater to different welding needs in various industries.</p></p>
