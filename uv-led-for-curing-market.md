<p><strong>The UV LED for Curing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>UV LED for curing market can be divided into three types based on the wavelength spectrum: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LED emit ultraviolet light in the 315-400 nm range, suitable for curing applications in inks, coatings, and adhesives. UV-B LED emit light in the 280-315 nm range, used for medical applications and water purification. UV-C LED emit light in the 100-280 nm range, ideal for disinfection and sterilization purposes. Each type of UV LED offers unique benefits and applications in the curing market.</p></p>
