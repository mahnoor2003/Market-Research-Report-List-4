<p><p>Loose flanges are commonly categorized into two main types - Raised Face (RF) and Flat Face (FF) flanges. RF flanges have a raised ring around the bore hole, allowing for better sealing under high pressure conditions. On the other hand, FF flanges have a flat surface, making them ideal for low pressure applications. Both types are widely used in various industries, with RF flanges being more common in high pressure systems while FF flanges are preferred for lower pressure applications.</p></p>
