<p><strong>The Medical Ultrasound Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Convex Type</li><li>Phased Array Type</li><li>Endocavitary Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical ultrasound probe market consists of various types including linear, convex, phased array, endocavitary, and others. Linear probes are used for high-resolution imaging of superficial structures, while convex probes are ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array probes allow for versatile scanning angles, making them suitable for cardiac and vascular imaging. Endocavitary probes are designed for intracavity imaging, such as in gynecology. Other probe types include specialty probes for specific applications, such as transesophageal echocardiography.</p></p>
