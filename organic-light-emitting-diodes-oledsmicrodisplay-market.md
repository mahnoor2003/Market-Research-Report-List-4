<p><p>Organic Light-emitting Diodes (OLEDs) Microdisplay technology is utilized in two main market types: Reflective Microdisplay and Transmissive Microdisplay. Reflective Microdisplay technology utilizes mirrors to reflect light towards the viewer, providing high contrast and visibility in bright environments. On the other hand, Transmissive Microdisplay technology allows light to pass through the display panel, resulting in vibrant colors and high resolution. Both market types offer unique advantages and cater to different applications based on their specific display characteristics.</p></p>
