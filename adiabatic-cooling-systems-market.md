<p><p>Adiabatic cooling systems are used for cooling purposes without the need for external energy input. The two main types of adiabatic cooling systems are dry air coolers and liquid coolers. Dry air coolers use ambient air for cooling without adding any moisture, while liquid coolers use water or other fluids to cool the air through evaporation. Both systems are energy-efficient and cost-effective options for cooling industrial and commercial spaces, providing effective temperature control while minimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
