<p><ul><li>Substrate Siscoloration Glasses</li><li>Film-Changing Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photochromic glasses come in two types - substrate discoloration glasses and film-changing glasses. Substrate discoloration glasses rely on the photochromic material being incorporated directly into the glass substrate, allowing for a more permanent color change. On the other hand, film-changing glasses feature a thin photochromic film that can be applied to existing lenses, providing a temporary color-changing effect. Both types of glasses offer the convenience of transitioning from clear to tinted lenses in response to UV exposure.</p></p>
