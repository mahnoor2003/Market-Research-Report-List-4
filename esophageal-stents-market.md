<p><p>Esophageal stents are medical devices used to keep the esophagus open in cases of strictures or blockages. There are two main types of esophageal stents in the market: Self-expandable Metallic Stents (SEMS) and Self-expanding Plastic Stents (SEPS). SEMS are typically made of metal and are flexible, allowing them to expand to the size of the esophagus. SEPS, on the other hand, are made of plastic and are also flexible but tend to have a shorter lifespan compared to SEMS.</p></p>
