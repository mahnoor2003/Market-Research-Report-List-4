<p><ul><li>Dry Type Lighting Capacitor</li><li>Oil Filled Type Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lighting capacitors are divided into two main types based on their construction: dry type and oil filled type capacitors. Dry type lighting capacitors contain a solid dielectric material, such as polypropylene, that prevents leakage of current and provides stable capacitance. On the other hand, oil filled type capacitors use an insulating oil to enhance performance and extend lifespan. Both types are widely used in lighting applications to improve power factor and reduce energy consumption.</p></p>
