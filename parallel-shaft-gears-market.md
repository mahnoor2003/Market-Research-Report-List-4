<p><p>Parallel shaft gears are commonly categorized into four main types: spur gears, rack & pinion gears, herringbone gears, and helical gears. Spur gears are the simplest type, providing straight-toothed gears for parallel shafts. Rack & pinion gears convert rotational motion into linear motion. Herringbone gears feature a unique double helical design to minimize noise and vibration. Helical gears have angled teeth for smoother and quieter operation. Each type of gear serves specific purposes based on the application and requirements of the system.</p></p>
