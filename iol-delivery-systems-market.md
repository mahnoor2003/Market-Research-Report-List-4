<p><p>IOL Delivery Systems are used in cataract surgeries to insert intraocular lenses into the eye. There are two main types of IOL Delivery Systems: Preloaded and Non-Preloaded. Preloaded systems come with the lens already loaded into the injector, making the procedure quicker and more efficient. On the other hand, Non-Preloaded systems require the surgeon to manually load the lens into the injector before insertion. Both systems offer their own advantages and cater to the different preferences of surgeons and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
