<p><p>Heated holding cabinets come in two main types based on their mobility: on casters and without casters. Heated holding cabinets on casters are designed for easy transportation and movement within a commercial kitchen or foodservice establishment. They are equipped with wheels so they can be easily rolled from one location to another. On the other hand, heated holding cabinets without casters are more stationary and are typically placed in a fixed location within a kitchen or facility. Both types serve the same purpose of keeping food warm and ready for serving.</p></p>
