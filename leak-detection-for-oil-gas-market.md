<p><p>Leak detection for the oil & gas industry includes various market types such as flow meters, infra-red detection systems, and fiber optic sensors. Flow meters measure the flow rate of liquids or gases, allowing for the detection of abnormal flow patterns that indicate a leak. Infra-red detection systems use infrared technology to identify gas leaks based on changes in temperature. Fiber optic sensors detect leaks by measuring changes in light reflection within pipelines. These technologies help ensure the safe and efficient operation of oil and gas facilities.</p></p>
