<p><p>Solvent recovery equipment market can be classified into two types: on-site solvent recovery equipment and off-site solvent recovery equipment. Off-site solvent recovery equipment, on the other hand, involves sending solvent waste to a separate facility for recovery and reuse. Both types of equipment help companies reduce waste and save costs by efficiently recovering and reusing solvents.</p></p>
