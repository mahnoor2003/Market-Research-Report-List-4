<p><p>Self-monitoring blood glucose (SMBG) devices are essential tools for individuals with diabetes to manage their condition. The market for SMBG devices includes three main types: strips, glucose meters, and lancets. Strips are disposable components used to collect a blood sample for testing. Glucose meters are devices that analyze the blood sample to provide a glucose level reading. Lancets are used to prick the skin for blood sample collection. These devices are crucial for maintaining optimal blood sugar levels and preventing complications related to diabetes.</p></p>
