<p><p>Search Engine Marketing Solutions offer various market types to advertisers based on their goals and budget. CPT (Cost Per Time) charges advertisers based on the time their ads are displayed on the search engine. CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) charges advertisers only when a specific action is performed, such as a purchase or sign-up. CPC (Cost Per Click) charges advertisers for each click on their ad. CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions) charges advertisers for every thousand times their ad is shown. Other markets include hybrid models or performance-based pricing.</p></p>
