<p><p>Seismic isolators are devices designed to reduce the shaking and vibrations experienced by buildings during earthquakes. There are several types of seismic isolators on the market, including Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB), Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB), High Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR), and others. NRB isolators use layers of rubber to absorb seismic energy, while LRB isolators use a combination of rubber and lead. HDR isolators have enhanced damping properties, providing better protection against earthquakes. Each type of isolator offers unique benefits for mitigating earthquake damage in structures.</p></p>
