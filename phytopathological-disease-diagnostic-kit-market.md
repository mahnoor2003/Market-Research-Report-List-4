<p><p>The Phytopathological Disease Diagnostic Kit market includes two main types: Serological Tests and Molecular Tests. Serological Tests detect the presence of antibodies or antigens in plant samples, allowing for the identification of specific pathogens. Molecular Tests, on the other hand, use techniques such as PCR to detect DNA or RNA sequences of pathogens in plant tissue. Both types of tests are essential tools for quickly and accurately diagnosing plant diseases, helping farmers effectively manage crop health and yield.</p></p>
