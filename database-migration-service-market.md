<p><strong>The Database Migration Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Live Migration</li><li>Cold Migration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Database Migration Service Market includes two main types: Live Migration and Cold Migration. </p><p>Live Migration involves transferring data from one database to another in real-time without disrupting ongoing operations. This type of migration is preferred for businesses that require continuous access to their data and cannot afford any downtime.</p><p>Cold Migration, on the other hand, involves shutting down the source database before transferring data to the target database. While this may result in some downtime, it is often faster and simpler than live migration, making it suitable for smaller databases or less critical systems.</p></p>
