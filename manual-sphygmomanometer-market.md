<p><strong>The Manual Sphygmomanometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aneroid Sphygmomanometer</li><li>Mercury Sphygmomanometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aneroid sphygmomanometers are manual blood pressure monitoring devices that use a dial gauge to measure blood pressure. They are portable, durable, and easy to use, making them suitable for home use. Mercury sphygmomanometers use a column of mercury to measure blood pressure and are considered the gold standard for accuracy. However, they are less commonly used due to the potential health and environmental risks associated with mercury. Both types are important tools for healthcare professionals in monitoring and managing blood pressure.</p></p>
