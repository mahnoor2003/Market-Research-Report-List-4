Gas-Water Circulation Type generators utilize a gas-water mixing system to create ultrafine bubbles, while Pressurized Dissolution Type generators dissolve gas under high pressure to produce ultrafine bubbles. Both types are used in various industries such as wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and agriculture for applications like water purification, oxygenation, and nutrient delivery. The choice of generator type depends on specific requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
