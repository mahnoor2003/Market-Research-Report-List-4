<p><ul><li>Grid-Connected Solar Microinverter</li><li>Grid-Off Solar Microinverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-Connected Solar Microinverters are used in systems that are connected to the utility grid, allowing excess energy generated by the solar panels to be fed back into the grid for credit or compensation. On the other hand, Grid-Off Solar Microinverters are designed for off-grid systems, where excess energy is stored in batteries for later use when solar energy generation is low. Both types of microinverters play a crucial role in maximizing the efficiency and flexibility of solar energy systems.</p></p>
