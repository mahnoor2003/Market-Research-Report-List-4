<p><p>Portable pocket currency detectors are commonly used to detect counterfeit currency. There are two main types of technology used in these devices: fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection works by illuminating the currency and detecting any fluorescent markings that are present on genuine currency. Magnetic detection, on the other hand, works by analyzing the magnetic properties of the currency to determine if it is genuine or counterfeit. Both types of technology are essential in the market for portable pocket currency detectors to ensure accurate and reliable results.</p></p>
