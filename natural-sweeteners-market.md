<p><ul><li>High Intensity</li><li>Low Intensity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural sweeteners can be categorized into two main types based on their intensity: high intensity sweeteners and low intensity sweeteners. High intensity sweeteners are several times sweeter than traditional table sugar, allowing for a much smaller amount to be used in products. Examples include stevia and monk fruit. On the other hand, low intensity sweeteners provide a similar level of sweetness to sugar but with fewer calories. Examples include honey and maple syrup. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and dietary needs in the natural sweeteners market.</p></p>
