<p><p>Automotive Super Swamper Tires come in two main types - Bias Tire and Radial Tire markets. Bias tires have multiple layers of fabric for increased strength and flexibility, making them suitable for heavy-duty off-road use. Radial tires have steel belts running from bead to bead for better traction and durability, making them ideal for highway driving. Bias tires are typically cheaper but less stable at high speeds, while radial tires offer improved performance and longer lifespan. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
