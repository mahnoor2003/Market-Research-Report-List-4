<p><p>High Purity Copper Foil market is divided into two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced by passing copper through a series of rollers to achieve the desired thickness and smooth surface finish. It is widely used in printed circuit boards and flexible electronics. On the other hand, Electrolytic Copper Foil is made through an electroplating process, resulting in a higher purity level. It is commonly used in batteries, electromagnetic shielding, and electrical equipment.</p></p>
