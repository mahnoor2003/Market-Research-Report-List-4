<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cementing Plugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Cementing Plug</li><li>Top Cementing Plug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bottom cementing plugs are used during the cementing process in oil and gas wells to separate the cement slurry from other fluids. They are placed at the bottom of the casing to prevent backflow of cement. Top cementing plugs are used in a similar manner, but they are placed at the top of the casing to ensure a proper seal and prevent contamination. Both types of cementing plugs play a crucial role in the successful completion of well cementing operations in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
