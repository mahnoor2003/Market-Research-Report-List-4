<p><p>Industrial clutches are essential components in various machinery and equipment for transmitting power. The market for industrial clutches is segmented into three main types: electromagnetic clutch, hydraulic clutch, and pneumatic clutch. Electromagnetic clutches utilize electromagnetic force to engage and disengage power transmission. Hydraulic clutches rely on hydraulic fluid for power transmission, providing smooth and precise operation. Pneumatic clutches use compressed air to control engagement and disengagement, offering quick response and high torque capacity. Each type of clutch caters to different industrial applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
