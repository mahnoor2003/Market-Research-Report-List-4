<p><ul><li>Passive RFID Transponders</li><li>Active RFID Transponders</li><li>Semi-Active RFID Transponders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID Transponders are categorized into three main types: Passive RFID Transponders, Active RFID Transponders, and Semi-Active RFID Transponders. Passive RFID Transponders do not have a power source and rely on the reader for power. Active RFID Transponders have their own power source and can transmit signals over longer distances. Semi-Active RFID Transponders also have their own power source but only use it to wake up and transmit data when activated by a reader. Each type has specific applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
