<p><p>Dialyzers are medical devices used for hemodialysis to remove waste and excess fluids from the blood of patients with kidney failure. There are three main types of dialyzers in the market: Hollow Fiber Dialyzer, Coil Tube Dialyzer, and Flat Type Dialyzer. Hollow Fiber dialyzers have a dense network of hollow fibers for blood filtration. Coil Tube dialyzers have a coiled design for efficient dialysis. Flat Type dialyzers have a flat membrane surface for blood purification. Each type offers different benefits and can be chosen based on patient needs.</p></p>
