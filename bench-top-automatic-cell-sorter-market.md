<p><p>Bench top automatic cell sorters are designed for separating different types of cells based on specific characteristics. Single cell sorters isolate individual cells for further analysis. Fluorescent activated cell sorters use fluorescent markers to identify and separate cells. Magnetic cell sorters utilize magnetic beads to label and separate cells. Buoyancy activated cell sorters separate cells based on their density differences. Each type of sorter has its own unique method of cell separation, catering to different research and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
