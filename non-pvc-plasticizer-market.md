<p><p>Non-PVC plasticizers are commonly categorized into four main types: Phthalates, Trimellitates, Aliphatic, and Benzoates. Phthalates are the most widely used type, known for their flexibility and durability. Trimellitates are preferred for their high-temperature tolerance. Aliphatic plasticizers are valued for their low volatility and migration resistance. Benzoates are gaining popularity for their non-toxic and environmentally friendly properties. Each type of plasticizer offers different benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
