<p><p>Audio sensors are devices that convert soundwaves into electrical signals for processing. There are two main types of audio sensors based on their output - Analog Output and Digital Output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals proportional to the sound intensity, while digital output sensors provide discrete binary signals indicating sound presence or absence. Both types cater to different applications, with analog sensors offering higher resolution and digital sensors providing easier interfacing with digital devices.</p></p>
