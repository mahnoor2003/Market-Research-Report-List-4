<p><p>RO water purifiers are broadly divided into two market types based on their installation method. Point-of-use (POU) RO water purifiers are installed at a specific point where water is consumed, such as kitchen sink. Point-of-entry (POE) RO water purifiers are installed at the main waterline of the house, providing purified water to the entire household. Both POU and POE RO water purifiers are popular choices for households looking to ensure safe and clean drinking water.</p></p>
