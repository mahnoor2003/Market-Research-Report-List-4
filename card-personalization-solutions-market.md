<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card personalization solutions market types include Dye Sub, Retransfer, and Inkjet technologies. Dye Sub involves transferring dye onto the card surface using heat, resulting in high quality, durable cards. Retransfer utilizes a two-step process to bond the image onto the card, offering edge-to-edge printing and enhanced security features. Inkjet technology directly prints the image onto the card, making it a cost-effective option for smaller batch printing. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages depending on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
