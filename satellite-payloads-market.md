<p><p>Satellite payloads market types can be categorized based on the orbits they operate in. LEO satellites are positioned at lower altitudes, offering faster transmission speeds and lower latencies. GEO satellites orbit at a fixed position relative to the Earth's surface, providing continuous coverage of a specific area. MEO satellites operate in between LEO and GEO orbits, offering a balance between coverage and latency. Each type of satellite payload serves different purposes based on their orbit characteristics and capabilities.</p></p>
