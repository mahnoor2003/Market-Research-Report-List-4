Optical cables are used to transmit data optically, while transceivers both transmit and receive optical signals. Optical multiplexers combine multiple optical signals into a single, high-speed signal. Optical attenuators are used to adjust the power level of optical signals. Additionally, the market includes other products that support the transmission of data using silicon photonics technology.</p></p>
