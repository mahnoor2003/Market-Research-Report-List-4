<p><p>Dental apex locators are devices used in endodontic procedures to determine the length of the tooth's root canal. There are two main types of apex locators - Alarm Type and Digital Readout. Alarm Type apex locators provide an audible alarm when the apex of the canal is reached, making it easier for the dentist to locate the apex. Digital Readout apex locators display the measurement of the canal's length on a digital screen, allowing for precise and accurate readings. Both types of apex locators are essential tools in modern endodontic practice.</p></p>
