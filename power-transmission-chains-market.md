<p><p>Power transmission chains refer to the various components used in the transmission of electrical power from generators to consumers. The market types include transformer, circuit breaker, isolator, insulator, arrestor, transmission line, and transmission tower. Transformers are used to change the voltage levels, circuit breakers protect the system from overloads, isolators ensure safety during maintenance, insulators prevent leakage of current, arrestors protect against lightning strikes, transmission lines carry the power, and transmission towers support the lines. Each component plays a crucial role in efficient power transmission.</p></p>
