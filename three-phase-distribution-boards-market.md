<p><p>Main Distribution Boards (MDBs) are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings to distribute power to various equipment and machinery. They typically contain circuit breakers, fuses, and other protective devices to ensure safe power distribution. Emergency Distribution Boards (EDBs) are similar to MDBs but are specifically designed to provide power during emergencies or power outages. They often include backup power sources such as generators or battery packs to ensure continued operation of essential equipment. Both types of distribution boards play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and safe power distribution in various settings.</p></p>
