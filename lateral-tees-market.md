<p><p>Lateral tees are commonly available in two market types: 45° and 90°. A 45° lateral tee is designed to allow flow in the horizontal direction at a 45-degree angle, making it ideal for applications where a gradual turn is needed. On the other hand, a 90° lateral tee directs flow perpendicularly, making it suitable for situations that require a sharp change in direction. Both types offer versatility and efficiency in piping systems, catering to various flow requirements with precision.</p></p>
