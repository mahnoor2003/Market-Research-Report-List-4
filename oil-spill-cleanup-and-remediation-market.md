<p><ul><li>Physical</li><li>Chemical & Biological</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical, chemical, and biological, and mechanical are the four main types of oil spill cleanup and remediation markets. Physical methods involve the use of booms, skimmers, and absorbent materials to contain and remove the oil from the water surface. Chemical methods use dispersants to break down oil into smaller droplets. Biological methods utilize microbes to biodegrade the oil. Mechanical methods involve the use of equipment such as pumps and vacuums to physically remove oil from the water. Each method has its own advantages and limitations in effectively cleaning up oil spills.</p></p>
