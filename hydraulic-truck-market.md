<p><p>Hydraulic truck markets can be divided into two types: Manual Hydraulic and Electronic Hydraulic. Manual Hydraulic systems rely on physical mechanisms like levers to control the truck's hydraulic functions, offering simplicity and durability. On the other hand, Electronic Hydraulic systems use electronic components to allow for remote operation and more precise control over the truck's hydraulics. While Manual Hydraulic systems are often preferred for their reliability, Electronic Hydraulic systems offer advanced features and convenience for operators.</p></p>
