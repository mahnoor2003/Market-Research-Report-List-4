<p><ul><li>Top Mounted</li><li>Side Mounted</li><li>Bottom Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paddle mixers are classified based on their mounting types - top mounted, side mounted, and bottom mounted. Top mounted paddle mixers have the paddles resting above the mixing chamber, allowing for efficient mixing with minimal heat generation. Side mounted paddle mixers have the paddles positioned on one side of the chamber, ideal for applications requiring gentle mixing. Bottom mounted paddle mixers have the paddles located at the bottom of the chamber, ensuring thorough mixing of materials. Each type suits different mixing requirements and industries.</p></p>
