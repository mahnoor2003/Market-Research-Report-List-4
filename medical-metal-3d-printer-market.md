<p><p>Medical metal 3D printers use different technologies for additive manufacturing, which include Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Filament Fabrication (FGF), Laser Metal Fusion (LMF), and others. FDM technology involves melting and extruding metal filament, while FGF uses bound metal filament. LMF uses a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder together layer by layer. Other technologies may include Electron Beam Melting (EBM) and Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS), each offering unique advantages and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
