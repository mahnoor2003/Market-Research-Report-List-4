<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrolyzer market consists of two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzer and PEM electrolyzer. Traditional alkaline electrolyzer uses potassium hydroxide solution as the electrolyte and operates at high temperatures, making it suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, PEM electrolyzer uses a polymer membrane as the electrolyte and operates at lower temperatures, making it more compact and efficient for smaller scale applications. Both types play a crucial role in the electrolysis process of splitting water into hydrogen and oxygen.</p></p>
