<p><p>Clinical Diagnosis Automation Market includes two main types: Modular Automation and Total Lab Automation. </p><p>Modular Automation involves the use of separate automated modules that can perform specific tasks within the diagnostic process. This allows for greater flexibility and customization in setting up a diagnostic system.</p><p>Total Lab Automation, on the other hand, involves the integration of multiple diagnostic processes into a single automated system. This streamlines the entire diagnostic process, reducing manual intervention and increasing efficiency. Both types of automation aim to improve accuracy, efficiency, and speed in clinical diagnosis.</p></p>
