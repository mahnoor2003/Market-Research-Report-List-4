<p><p>Dysphagia management market can be divided into two main types: High (Oropharangeal) Dysphagia and Low (Esophageal) Dysphagia. High dysphagia involves difficulties with swallowing in the mouth and throat areas, while low dysphagia involves issues in the esophagus. Each type requires different approaches to management and treatment. The market for high dysphagia may focus on oral exercises, diet modification, and swallowing therapy, while the market for low dysphagia may involve medications, endoscopic treatments, or even surgery.</p></p>
