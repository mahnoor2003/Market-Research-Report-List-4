<p><p>Cardiopulmonary autotransfusion devices are medical devices used to collect and reinfuse a patient's own blood during cardiac and thoracic surgeries. On-Pump Transfusion Devices are used during surgeries where the heart is supported by a heart-lung machine, while Off-Pump Transfusion Devices are used in surgeries performed without the use of a heart-lung machine. Both types of devices help reduce the need for donor blood transfusions, decrease the risk of infection and complications, and promote faster recovery for patients undergoing cardiac and thoracic surgeries.</p></p>
