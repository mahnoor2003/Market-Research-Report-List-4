Round balers create cylindrical bales by rolling the crop material into a compact shape, while square balers compress the crop material into square-shaped bales. Both types of balers are used in agriculture to efficiently collect and store hay, straw, and other crops for feeding livestock or selling as feed. The choice between round and square balers often depends on factors such as crop type, storage space, and desired bale shape.</p></p>
