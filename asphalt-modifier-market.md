<p><p>Asphalt modifiers can be categorized into three main types: physical modifiers, chemical modifiers, and other market modifiers. Physical modifiers, such as polymers and fibers, aim to enhance the mechanical properties of asphalt mixtures. Chemical modifiers, like anti-aging agents and adhesion promoters, work to improve the durability and performance of asphalt. The other market modifiers consist of additives such as crumb rubber, wax, and sulfur, which are used to enhance specific properties of asphalt mixtures. These modifiers play a crucial role in enhancing the overall quality and longevity of asphalt pavements.</p></p>
