<p><p>Medical 3D bioprinting market includes various types such as Stereolithography (SLA), Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM), and others. SLA uses photosensitive polymers to create precise structures. FDM involves extruding thermoplastic materials layer by layer. LOM uses layers of adhesive-coated paper or plastic to build the final product. Other types may include Inkjet 3D printing and Bioextrusion. Each type of bioprinting technology offers unique advantages and is used for different applications within the medical field.</p></p>
