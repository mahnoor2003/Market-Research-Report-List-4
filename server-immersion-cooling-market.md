<p><strong>The Server Immersion Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-phase</li><li>Two-phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Server Immersion Cooling Market Types include Single-phase and Two-phase cooling systems. </p><p>Single-phase cooling systems use dielectric fluids such as mineral oil or synthetic fluids to transfer heat away from servers. These systems are simpler and less expensive than Two-phase cooling systems, but may not be as efficient in cooling high-performance servers.</p><p>Two-phase cooling systems use a refrigerant or other phase-changing fluid that evaporates to absorb heat and condenses to release heat. These systems tend to be more efficient in cooling high-performance servers but are typically more complex and expensive than Single-phase systems.</p></p>
