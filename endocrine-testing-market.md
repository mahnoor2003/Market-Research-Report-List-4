<p><p>Endocrine testing is a crucial component of diagnosing and managing endocrine disorders. Two main types of techniques used in endocrine testing are tandem mass spectrometry and immunoassay. Tandem mass spectrometry is a highly sensitive and specific method that analyzes biomolecules based on their mass and chemical properties. Immunoassay, on the other hand, utilizes antibodies to detect and measure specific hormones in the blood. Both techniques play a vital role in accurately diagnosing endocrine disorders and monitoring hormonal levels in patients.</p></p>
