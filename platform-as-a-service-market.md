<p><p>Platform as a Service (PaaS) market types include Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud PaaS solutions are hosted by third-party providers on a shared infrastructure accessible to multiple users over the Internet. Private Cloud PaaS solutions are hosted on a dedicated infrastructure for a single organization, offering greater security and control. Hybrid Cloud PaaS solutions combine elements of both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both while maintaining flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
