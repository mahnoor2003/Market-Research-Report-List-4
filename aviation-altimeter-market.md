<p><p>Aviation altimeters are essential instruments for measuring altitude in aircraft. The types of aviation altimeters include barometric altimeters, which use atmospheric pressure to determine altitude; radio altimeters, which use radio waves to measure distance from the ground; GNSS altimeters, which utilize satellite navigation systems like GPS and Galileo; and laser altimeters, which use laser pulses to calculate altitude. Each type of altimeter has its own advantages and applications, providing pilots with accurate altitude information for safe and efficient flight operations.</p></p>
