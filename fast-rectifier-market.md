<p><ul><li>0V - 1.0V</li><li>Above 1.0V - 1.5V</li><li>Above 1.5V</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fast rectifiers are categorized based on the forward voltage drop. The 0V - 1.0V market typically includes Schottky diodes which have low forward voltage drop and fast switching speed. The Above 1.0V - 1.5V market includes silicon diodes with slightly higher forward voltage drop compared to Schottky diodes. The Above 1.5V market consists of specialized rectifiers like ultrafast or avalanche diodes with even higher forward voltage drop. Each market segment caters to different applications based on the specific requirements of voltage drop and switching speed.</p></p>
