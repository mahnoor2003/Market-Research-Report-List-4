<p><p>Drywall mud, a key component in finishing drywall surfaces, comes in two main types: quick-setting or "hot" mud and pre-mixed drywall mud. Quick-setting mud is ideal for projects requiring a fast turnaround, as it dries quickly and allows for multiple coats in a single day. Pre-mixed drywall mud, on the other hand, is ready to use straight out of the container, making it convenient for smaller jobs or for those who prefer a simpler application process. Both types offer different advantages based on the needs of the project at hand.</p></p>
