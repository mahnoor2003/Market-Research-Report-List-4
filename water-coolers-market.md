<p><p>Water coolers are available in two main market types: bottled water coolers and plumbed-in water coolers. Bottled water coolers require the manual installation of water bottles, which are typically either delivered or purchased separately. On the other hand, plumbed-in water coolers are connected directly to a water supply, eliminating the need for water bottles. Both types offer convenient access to cold and hot water, but the choice between them depends on individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
