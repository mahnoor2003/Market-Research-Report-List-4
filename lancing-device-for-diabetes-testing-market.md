<p><p>Lancing devices are essential tools for people with diabetes to prick their skin to obtain blood samples for glucose testing. They come in two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable. Adjustable lancing devices allow users to control the depth of the needle penetration, which is useful for individuals with varying skin thickness. On the other hand, non-adjustable lancing devices have a fixed depth setting, making them simpler to use but may not be suitable for all skin types. Both types of devices are crucial for accurate and painless blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
