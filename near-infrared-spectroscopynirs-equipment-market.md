<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) equipment market includes various types such as FT-NIR spectroscopy, dispersive NIR spectroscopy, and other methods like AOTF and filter-based spectroscopy. FT-NIR spectroscopy utilizes Fourier transform technology for high-resolution measurements, while dispersive NIR spectroscopy separates light into different wavelengths for analysis. Other methods like AOTF and filter-based spectroscopy use specific components to filter and detect near-infrared light. Each type of NIRS equipment offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and agriculture, and chemicals.</p></p>
