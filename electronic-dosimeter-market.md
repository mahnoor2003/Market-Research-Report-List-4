<p><p>The electronic dosimeter market is segmented into different types, such as TLD (thermoluminescent dosimeters), OSL (optically stimulated luminescence) dosimeters, and others. TLD dosimeters measure ionizing radiation by detecting light emitted when the dosimeter is heated. OSL dosimeters use a different method, measuring radiation exposure by detecting light emitted when the dosimeter is stimulated with light. Other types of electronic dosimeters may utilize different technologies or mechanisms to measure radiation levels accurately. These various types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
