<p><p>Vibration sensors are commonly categorized into four main types: Piezoresistive, Strain Gauge, Variable Capacitance, and Tri-Axial sensors. Piezoresistive sensors measure the electrical resistance changes due to mechanical stress. Strain Gauge sensors detect changes in resistance as a material deforms. Variable Capacitance sensors monitor changes in capacitance as the distance between plates changes. Tri-Axial sensors measure vibration in three dimensions. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
