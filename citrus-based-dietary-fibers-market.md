<p><p>Citrus based dietary fibers are classified into three main types: pectin, hemicellulose, and cellulose. Pectin is a soluble fiber that forms a gel-like substance in the digestive tract, aiding in digestion and maintaining regular bowel movements. Hemicellulose is a complex carbohydrate that supports digestive health by acting as a prebiotic, promoting the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. Cellulose, on the other hand, is an insoluble fiber that adds bulk to the stool and helps prevent constipation. Each type of citrus-based fiber offers unique health benefits for consumers.</p></p>
