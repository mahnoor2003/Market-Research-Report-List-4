<p><strong>The Catalysts for Polyolefins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Metallocene Catalyst</li><li>Dinuclear Metallocene Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Normal metallocene catalysts are single-site catalysts that consist of a metal atom bound to a cyclopentadienyl ligand. They are widely used in the production of polyolefins due to their high activity and selectivity. Dinuclear metallocene catalysts, on the other hand, consist of two metal atoms bridged by a ligand. These catalysts are known for their improved polymer properties and control over molecular weight distribution. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in the polyolefins market by enabling the production of high-quality plastics with specific properties.</p></p>
