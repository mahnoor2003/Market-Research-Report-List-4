<p><ul><li>Zone VRI</li><li>Sector VRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Rate Irrigation Technology Market Types include Zone VRI and Sector VRI. Zone VRI divides a field into smaller zones based on soil type, topography, or other factors, allowing for precise water application. Sector VRI involves dividing a field into pie-shaped sections, each with its own irrigation prescription. Both types of VRI technology allow for more efficient use of water and resources by tailoring irrigation levels to specific areas within a field, resulting in improved crop yield and water conservation.</p></p>
