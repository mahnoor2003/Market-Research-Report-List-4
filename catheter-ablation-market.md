<p><p>Catheter ablation market offers different types of procedures for treating various cardiac arrhythmias. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat to destroy abnormal heart tissue, while laser/light ablation uses energy from lasers or light to achieve the same result. Cryoablation involves freezing the tissue, microwave ablation uses electromagnetic waves, and hydrothermal ablation relies on heated water. Other types of ablation procedures may include ultrasound-guided ablation or electroporation ablation. Each method targets abnormal tissue in the heart to restore normal heart rhythms and improve the patient's quality of life.</p></p>
