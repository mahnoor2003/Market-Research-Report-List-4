<p><p>Desoldering tools can be classified into four main types - Desoldering Pumps, Desoldering Stations, Desoldering Guns, and Others. Desoldering pumps are manual tools used to remove solder from electronic components. Desoldering stations are more advanced with temperature control and suction features. Desoldering guns use hot air to melt solder for removal. The "Others" category includes specialized tools such as wick braid and solder suckers. Each type of desoldering tool offers unique features and benefits for precision solder removal in electronic repairs.</p></p>
