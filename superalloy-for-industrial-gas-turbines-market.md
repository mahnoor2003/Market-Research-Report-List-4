<p><p>Superalloys for industrial gas turbines are categorized into three main types based on their primary alloying elements: Fe based, Ni based, and Co based. Fe-based superalloys are mainly composed of iron and are known for their high strength and oxidation resistance. Ni-based superalloys, on the other hand, contain nickel as their primary alloying element and are favored for their excellent high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance. Co-based superalloys are characterized by their cobalt content and are used for their superior hot corrosion resistance and thermal stability in extreme conditions.</p></p>
