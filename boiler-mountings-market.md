<p><p>Boiler mountings are essential components that ensure the safe and efficient operation of boilers. The Water Level Indicator is used to monitor the water level inside the boiler. The Safety Valve releases excess steam to prevent pressure build-up. Pressure Gauge measures the pressure inside the boiler. The Steam Stop Valve controls the flow of steam. The Feed Check Valve regulates the supply of water into the boiler. The Main Hole allows for inspection and maintenance. Other mountings may include Blowdown Valve, Fusible Plug, and Manifold.</p></p>
