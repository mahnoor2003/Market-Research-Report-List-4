<p><p>Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) market includes three main types: Non-Powered Air Respirators (NPAR), Powered Air Respirators (PAR), and Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA). NPARs are simple masks filtering out particles and gases. PARs use a battery-powered fan to provide filtered air. SCBA is more advanced, offering a portable air supply for hazardous environments. Each type of RPE caters to different levels of respiratory protection, allowing users to choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs and working conditions.</p></p>
