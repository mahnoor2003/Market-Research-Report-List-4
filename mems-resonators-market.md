<p><p>MEMS resonators are small, highly sensitive devices used in various applications like smartphones, IoT devices, and wearables. There are two main types of MEMS resonators based on their mode of operation: Series Resonance Type and Parallel Resonance Type. Series resonators are known for their high quality factor and narrow bandwidth, making them suitable for precision frequency control applications. On the other hand, parallel resonators offer lower impedance and wider bandwidth, making them ideal for applications where high power handling is required.</p></p>
