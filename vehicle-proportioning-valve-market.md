<p><strong>The Vehicle Proportioning Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Master Cylinder-Mounted Proportioning Valves</li><li>Load Sensing Proportioning Valves</li><li>Combination Valve-Mounted Proportioning Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle proportioning valves are crucial components in a vehicle's braking system that help control the pressure of the brake fluid distributed to the front and rear brakes. There are three main types of proportioning valves: Master Cylinder-Mounted Proportioning Valves, which are mounted near the master cylinder, Load Sensing Proportioning Valves, which adjust pressure based on vehicle load, and Combination Valve-Mounted Proportioning Valves, which combine various functions like metering, proportioning, and pressure differential control in one unit. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring safe and efficient braking performance.</p></p>
