<p><p>Supersonic flowmeters are utilized in various industries for accurate measurement of flow rates. The market is segmented into three types based on their installation methods: inline, clamp-on, and insertion. Inline flowmeters are directly installed within the pipeline, offering precise measurement without disrupting flow. Clamp-on flowmeters are mounted externally on the pipeline, making them easy to install and suitable for temporary measurements. Insertion flowmeters are inserted into the pipeline through a fitting, providing accurate readings for larger pipes.</p></p>
