<p><p>Breast-shaped tissue expanders are medical devices used in breast reconstruction surgeries. Dual-port tissue expanders have two injection ports for injecting saline solution to gradually expand the expander. One-port tissue expanders have only one injection port. Both types allow for controlled expansion of the breast tissue before permanent implants are inserted. Dual-port expanders may offer more flexibility in filling and adjusting the size, while one-port expanders are simpler in design and may be more convenient for some patients.</p></p>
