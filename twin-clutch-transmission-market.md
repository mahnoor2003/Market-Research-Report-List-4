<p><ul><li>Wet Multiplate Clutches</li><li>Dry Single-Plate Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin-clutch transmission market can be classified into two types based on the type of clutches used: Wet Multiplate Clutches and Dry Single-Plate Clutches. Wet multiplate clutches use oil to cool and lubricate the clutches, allowing for smoother operation and increased lifespan. On the other hand, dry single-plate clutches do not use oil, resulting in a simpler design and lower maintenance costs. Both types offer different benefits and cater to various consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
