<p><p>Polyurethane non-woven fabric market types include spunbond, spunlace, needle punch, meltblown, wet laid, and other specialized varieties. Spunbond non-woven fabric is formed by bonding fibers together through heat and pressure. Spunlace fabric is made by entangling fibers using water jets. Needle punch fabric is created by punching fibers together mechanically. Meltblown fabric involves blowing fibers onto a moving belt. Wet laid fabric is formed by mixing fibers in water and then drying them. Other specialized markets include unique non-woven fabric types catering to specific applications.</p></p>
