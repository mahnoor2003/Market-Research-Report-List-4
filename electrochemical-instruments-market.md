<p><p>Electrochemical instruments market is classified into various types based on their functions. Potentiometry measures the voltage difference between two electrodes, while voltammetry analyzes current as a function of applied potential. Coulometry quantifies the amount of substance through measuring the amount of electricity passed during a reaction. Other types include amperometry, conductometry, and impedance spectroscopy. Each type plays a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety, offering precise and reliable analysis of chemical compounds.</p></p>
