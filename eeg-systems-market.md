<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EEG systems include different types such as conventional EEG, video EEG, and dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG systems are used to monitor brain activity through electrodes attached to the scalp. Video EEG systems provide a combination of video recording with EEG data to capture and analyze the patient's behavior during seizures. Dynamic EEG systems offer real-time monitoring of brain activity, allowing for better analysis of changes in neural function. Each type of EEG system serves a unique purpose in diagnosing and treating neurological conditions.</p></p>
