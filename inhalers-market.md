<p><strong>The Inhalers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metered Dose Inhalers</li><li>Dry Powder Inhalers</li><li>Nasal Inhalers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) are portable devices that deliver a precise amount of medication in aerosol form. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) are breath-activated devices that deliver medication in powder form. Nasal inhalers are devices that deliver medication directly to the nasal passages. Other types of inhalers in the market include nebulizers, which deliver medication in mist form, and spacers, which help improve the effectiveness of MDIs. Each type of inhaler has its own advantages and is suitable for different patients depending on their condition and preferences.</p></p>
