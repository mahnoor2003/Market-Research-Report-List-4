<p><strong>The Neurovascular Guidewires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Guide Wire</li><li>Wrapped Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neurovascular guidewires are used in minimally invasive procedures to navigate through blood vessels. Solid guide wires consist of a single solid wire, providing greater support and durability for complex procedures. Wrapped guide wires are made of a thin wire wrapped around a core wire, offering flexibility and maneuverability in tortuous vessels. Both types play a crucial role in guiding catheters and other devices to target areas within the vascular system, aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of various neurovascular conditions.</p></p>
