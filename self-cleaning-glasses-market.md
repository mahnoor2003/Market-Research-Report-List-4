<p><p>Self-cleaning glasses market consists of two main types of coatings: hydrophobic and hydrophilic. Hydrophobic coatings repel water and other liquids, preventing dirt and grime from sticking to the surface. On the other hand, hydrophilic coatings attract water, causing it to spread and carry away dirt particles. Both coatings help keep the glass clean and clear, reducing the need for frequent cleaning and maintenance. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
