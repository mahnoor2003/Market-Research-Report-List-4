<p><p>Automotive acrylic sheets are transparent, lightweight, and impact-resistant materials used in various applications in the automotive industry. The global market for automotive acrylic sheets is segmented into two main types: extruded acrylic sheet and cast acrylic sheet. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced by continuously pushing acrylic mass through a forming die, resulting in a more economical option with lower optical quality. On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are manufactured by pouring liquid acrylic into a mold and allowing it to harden, offering higher strength and better clarity but at a higher cost.</p></p>
