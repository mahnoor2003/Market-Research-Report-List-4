<p><p>Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) Machines are classified into three main market types based on the diameter of the drill: Large-diameter HDD, Medium-diameter HDD, and Small-diameter HDD. Large-diameter HDD machines are used for drilling holes with diameters exceeding 24 inches, suitable for major infrastructure projects. Medium-diameter HDD machines are used for drilling holes with diameters ranging from 12 to 24 inches, commonly used in utilities and pipeline installations. Small-diameter HDD machines are used for drilling holes with diameters less than 12 inches, typically for residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
