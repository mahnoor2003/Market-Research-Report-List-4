<p><p>Biological Safety Cabinets are classified into different types based on their design and level of protection. Class II Type A cabinets provide personnel, product, and environmental protection, with recirculated air. Class II Type B cabinets offer enhanced protection by exhausting air to the outside through HEPA filters. Class III Type cabinets provide the highest level of containment, with all exhaust air being filtered to prevent any contaminants from escaping. Each type of cabinet is designed to suit different levels of biological containment requirements.</p></p>
