<p><p>Chemical agricultural colorants market includes two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble in water and are used to color liquids, while pigments are insoluble and are used to color solid materials. Dyes are more versatile and provide a wider range of colors, while pigments offer greater stability and resistance to fading. Both dyes and pigments play a crucial role in enhancing the visual appeal of agricultural products, such as fertilizers, pesticides, and seeds.</p></p>
