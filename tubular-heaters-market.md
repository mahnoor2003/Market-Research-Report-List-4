<p><p>Tubular heaters come in two main types: single-ended and double-ended. Single-ended tubular heaters have one end connected to a power source, while the other end is sealed. These heaters are typically used in applications where heating objects from one direction is sufficient. On the other hand, double-ended tubular heaters have both ends connected to a power source. These heaters are ideal for applications requiring even heat distribution and are commonly used in industrial processes.</p></p>
