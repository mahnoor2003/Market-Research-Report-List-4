<p><p>Amorphous Core Transformers are classified into two main types - Oil-Immersed and Dry-Type. Oil-Immersed Amorphous Core Transformers are filled with insulating oil to provide cooling and insulation, making them suitable for high-power applications. On the other hand, Dry-Type Amorphous Core Transformers do not require oil for cooling, making them more environmentally friendly and safer for indoor use. Both types of transformers offer improved energy efficiency and reduced losses compared to traditional transformers, making them a popular choice for power distribution systems.</p></p>
