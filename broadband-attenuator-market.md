<p><p>Broadband attenuators are devices used to reduce the intensity of a signal without distorting its waveform. There are two main types of broadband attenuators in the market: manual and electric. Manual attenuators require physical adjustment of the attenuation level, while electric attenuators can be controlled remotely. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications. Manual attenuators are often preferred for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while electric attenuators are more versatile and offer precise control over attenuation levels.</p></p>
