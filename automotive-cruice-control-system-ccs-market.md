<p><p>Automotive Cruise Control System (CCS) comes in different types such as Mechanical Guided CCS, Electronic Throttle CCS, and Multifunction CCS. Mechanical Guided CCS uses a mechanical linkage to maintain a constant speed, Electronic Throttle CCS uses electronic sensors to control the throttle position, and Multifunction CCS offers additional features like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assistance. These systems help improve driving comfort and convenience by allowing drivers to maintain a set speed without constant foot pressure on the accelerator pedal.</p></p>
