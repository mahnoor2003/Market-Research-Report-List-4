<p><p>Regenerative heat exchangers are used to recover heat from exhaust gases and transfer it to incoming air or gas streams. The types of regenerative heat exchangers include tubular type, regenerative air preheaters, and regenerators. Tubular type heat exchangers use tube bundles to transfer heat, regenerative air preheaters use rotating heat exchanger elements, and regenerators use ceramic media to store and transfer heat. Each type serves the purpose of improving energy efficiency and reducing fuel consumption in industrial processes.</p></p>
