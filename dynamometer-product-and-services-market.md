<p><p>Dynamometer products and services are commonly classified into three types: Absorption dynamometers, Transmission dynamometers, and Motoring dynamometers. Absorption dynamometers measure the power of an engine by absorbing it and converting it into heat or electricity. Transmission dynamometers measure power by applying a load to the engine's output shaft. Motoring dynamometers simulate real-world conditions by applying a load to the engine shaft to measure power output. Each type of dynamometer has its own advantages and applications in the automotive and engineering industries.</p></p>
