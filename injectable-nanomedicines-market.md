<p><p>Injectable nanomedicines are a growing market with various types of formulations available. Liposomes are spherical vesicles that can encapsulate drugs for targeted delivery. Micelles are self-assembled nanoparticles that solubilize drugs in aqueous environments. Nanocrystals are small particles with high drug loading capacity. Polymeric nanoparticles are made of biodegradable polymers for sustained drug release. Other types of injectable nanomedicines include dendrimers, metallic nanoparticles, and quantum dots, each offering unique advantages for drug delivery and targeting. These innovative formulations are revolutionizing the way drugs are administered and are expected to drive significant growth in the market.</p></p>
