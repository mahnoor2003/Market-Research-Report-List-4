<p><p>Waste Heat Recovery System Market comprises two main types: Steam Rankine Cycle and Organic Rankine Cycle. In Steam Rankine Cycle, waste heat is used to generate steam, which drives a turbine to produce electricity. On the other hand, Organic Rankine Cycle uses organic fluids to convert waste heat into mechanical power. Both systems aim to capture and utilize waste heat from industrial processes to increase energy efficiency and reduce greenhouse gas emissions. Despite some differences in operation, both technologies have been widely adopted in various industries for their sustainability benefits.</p></p>
