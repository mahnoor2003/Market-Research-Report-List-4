<p><ul><li>Metal Thick Film Heater</li><li>Ceramic Thick Film Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick film heaters are divided into two main types - metal thick film heaters and ceramic thick film heaters. Metal thick film heaters are constructed with a metal substrate and a thick film heating element applied on top, offering high thermal conductivity and fast heat transfer. On the other hand, ceramic thick film heaters are made with a ceramic substrate and provide excellent thermal stability and uniform heating. Both types of heaters are commonly used in various applications such as automotive, medical devices, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
