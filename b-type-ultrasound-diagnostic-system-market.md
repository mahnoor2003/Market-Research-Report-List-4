<p><strong>The B type Ultrasound Diagnostic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type are three common types of ultrasound diagnostic systems in the market. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a curved transducer to obtain a wider field of view, making it suitable for imaging organs like the heart. Linear scanning uses a straight transducer for high-resolution imaging of structures close to the surface, such as blood vessels. Mixed type systems combine both fanshaped and linear scanning capabilities, providing versatility in imaging different types of tissues and organs.</p></p>
