<p><p>Medium viscosity bone cement is a type of cement used in orthopedic procedures, particularly in joint replacements such as hip and knee replacements. It has a consistency that is thicker than low viscosity bone cement but not as thick as high viscosity bone cement. This makes it easier to handle during surgery while still providing adequate support and fixation of implants.</p><p>The medium viscosity bone cement market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders and the rising number of orthopedic surgeries globally. The market is also being propelled by advancements in material technology, which are leading to the development of improved formulations of bone cement with better mechanical properties and biocompatibility.</p><p>Key trends in the medium viscosity bone cement market include the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures, the growing adoption of bone cement in trauma surgeries, and the increasing focus on patient outcomes and quality of life post-surgery. Additionally, the market is witnessing a shift towards the use of antibiotic-loaded bone cement to prevent infections in orthopedic surgeries.</p><p>Overall, the medium viscosity bone cement market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as the aging population, technological advancements, and the increasing prevalence of orthopedic conditions worldwide.</p></p>
