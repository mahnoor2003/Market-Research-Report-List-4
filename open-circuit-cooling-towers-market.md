<p><strong>The Open Circuit Cooling Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Counterflow</li><li>Crossflow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open circuit cooling towers are commonly classified into two main types: counterflow and crossflow. </p><p>Counterflow cooling towers have the hot water entering at the top and flowing downwards, while the cool air enters from the bottom and flows upwards, creating a counterflow pattern for efficient heat exchange.</p><p>Crossflow cooling towers have the hot water flowing horizontally across the fill media, while the cool air flows vertically, perpendicular to the water flow, allowing for effective heat dissipation.</p><p>Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
