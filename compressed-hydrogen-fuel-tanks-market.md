<p><p>Compressed hydrogen fuel tanks are categorized into five different types based on their design and material composition: Type I, Type II, Type III, Type IV, and Type V. Type I tanks are made of steel and are the heaviest and least efficient. Type II tanks use a metal liner with a composite wrapping. Type III tanks feature a metal liner and a composite layer. Type IV tanks have a fully composite structure. Type V tanks are the lightest and most efficient, constructed entirely of composite materials.</p></p>
