<p><p>Nickel-Aluminum Bronze (NAB) valves are available in various types including Check Valves, Ball Valves, Butterfly Valves, and others. Check Valves are designed to only allow fluid to flow in one direction, preventing backflow. Ball Valves use a perforated and pivoting ball to control the flow of liquid. Butterfly Valves use a circular disc to regulate flow. Other NAB valve types may include gate valves, globe valves, or diaphragm valves, each with unique functions and advantages in different applications.</p></p>
