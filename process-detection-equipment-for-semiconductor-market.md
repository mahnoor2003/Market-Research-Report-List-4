<p><p>Process detection equipment for the semiconductor market includes front track detection and back track detection. Front track detection monitors the progression of the semiconductor manufacturing process from the beginning, ensuring proper alignment and movement of materials. Back track detection, on the other hand, focuses on detecting any issues or defects that may have occurred later in the process and allows for quick identification and resolution of problems. Both types of detection equipment are essential for maintaining quality control and efficient production in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
