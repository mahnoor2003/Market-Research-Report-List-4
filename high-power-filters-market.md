<p><p>High Power Filters market is segmented into three types- Passive Filter, Active Filter, and Others. Passive filters use only passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors to filter out unwanted frequencies. Active filters, on the other hand, use active components such as transistors and op-amps to amplify and process signals before filtering them. The Others category includes specialized filters like digital filters or custom-designed filters tailored to specific applications. These different types cater to various requirements and preferences in high power filtering applications.</p></p>
