<p><p>Rod Ends market can be classified into two types based on the type of threads: Internal Threads and External Threads. In the Internal Threads market, the rod end has threads on the inside, allowing it to be screwed onto an external threaded rod or stud. On the other hand, in the External Threads market, the rod end has threads on the outside, enabling it to be attached to an internally threaded housing or mount. These two types cater to different applications and provide versatility in connecting rods in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
