<p><p>Valve actuator systems are available in different types depending on the method used to control valve movement. Manual actuators require physical effort to operate, while pneumatic actuators use air pressure. Hydraulic actuators utilize fluid pressure, and electric actuators rely on electrical power. Spring actuators use the force generated by springs to control valve movement. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to various industry needs for precise, efficient valve control.</p></p>
