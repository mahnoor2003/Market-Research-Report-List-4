<p><p>Handling Robot market types include Articulated Material Handling Robots, SCARA Material Handling Robots, and Parallel Material Handling Robots. Articulated robots use rotary joints to provide a wide range of motion, making them ideal for complex tasks. SCARA robots have high speed and precision, making them suitable for tasks requiring fast and accurate movements. Parallel robots use multiple arms working together to handle heavy loads with speed and precision. Each type of robot offers unique capabilities to meet different material handling needs.</p></p>
