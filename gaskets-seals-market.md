<p><p>The gaskets & seals market can be broadly categorized into metallic gaskets (such as screw and bolts), non-metallic gaskets (including nuts), body seals, shaft seals, and molded seals. Metallic gaskets are used for high-pressure applications, while non-metallic gaskets are preferred for their chemical resistance. Body seals are essential for preventing leakage in automotive and industrial equipment, while shaft seals are crucial for maintaining lubrication in rotating machinery. Molded seals are customized solutions for specific applications, providing effective sealing and protection.</p></p>
