<p><p>Hot foil stampers are available in three main types - manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual hot foil stampers require the operator to manually feed the material and operate the machine. Semi-automatic hot foil stampers have some automated features, such as feeding the material, but still require manual operation for other functions. Automatic hot foil stampers are fully automated, with the machine handling all aspects of the stamping process. Each type caters to different needs and production sizes in the market.</p></p>
