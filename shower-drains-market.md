<p><p>The shower drains market offers two main types: point drains and linear drains. Point drains are designed as a single point in the shower floor where water is collected and drained away. On the other hand, linear drains are longer and sleeker, typically installed along one side or the center of the shower floor to collect water efficiently. Both types offer different design options and functionality to accommodate various shower layouts and preferences in the market.</p></p>
