<p><p>The vacuum switching valve market consists of various types of valves, including butterfly valves, gate valves, isolation valves, throttling valves, slit/door valves, angle/inline valves, all metal valves, pendulum valves, and others. Butterfly valves control the flow by a disk rotating on a shaft. Gate valves are operated by a moving gate to block or allow flow. Isolation valves are used to completely shut off flow. Throttling valves regulate flow by adjusting the opening. Slit/door valves control flow through a slit or door opening. Angle/inline valves direct flow at specific angles. All metal valves are made entirely of metal for durability. Pendulum valves operate with a swinging door mechanism.</p></p>
