<p><p>High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) market includes various types such as Partition Chromatography, Normal-phase Chromatography, Displacement Chromatography, Reversed-phase Chromatography (RPC), Size-exclusion Chromatography, Ion-exchange Chromatography, and Bioaffinity Chromatography. Partition Chromatography separates compounds based on their partition between stationary and mobile phases. Normal-phase Chromatography separates compounds by polarity. Displacement Chromatography uses a displacer to displace components from the stationary phase. RPC separates compounds based on hydrophobic interactions. Size-exclusion Chromatography separates compounds by size. Ion-exchange Chromatography separates compounds based on ionic interactions. Bioaffinity Chromatography separates compounds based on biological affinity.</p></p>
