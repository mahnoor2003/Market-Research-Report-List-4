<p><p>Intelligent Pipeline Pigging market is categorized into three types - MFL Type, UT Type, and Others. MFL Type utilizes magnetic flux leakage technology to detect and measure metal loss in pipelines. UT Type uses ultrasonic technology for thickness measurement and flaw detection. Others refer to various innovative technologies used for pipeline inspection such as smart pigs and chemical cleaning pigs. These different types cater to diverse pipeline maintenance needs and requirements in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
