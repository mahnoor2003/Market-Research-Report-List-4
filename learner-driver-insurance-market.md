<p><ul><li>Own Car</li><li>Someone else's Car</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Learner driver insurance can be categorized into two main market types: Own Car market and Someone else's Car market. In the Own Car market, learners can insure the car they will be practicing in under their own name. This allows them to build up their own no-claims bonus and potentially reduce future insurance costs. In the Someone else's Car market, learners can be added as a named driver on another person's insurance policy, allowing them to drive that person's car with coverage.</p></p>
