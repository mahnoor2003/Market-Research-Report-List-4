<p><p>Roof drains market types include the Sill Type, Parapet Type, Flow Control Type, Overflow Type, and Others. Sill Type drains are installed on the roof's perimeter, while Parapet Type drains are built into the parapet walls. Flow Control Type drains regulate water flow, and Overflow Type drains prevent water build-up during heavy rainfall. Other types of roof drains may include combination drain types or specialty drains designed for specific roofing systems or conditions. Each type serves a specific function in efficiently managing water drainage from the roof.</p></p>
