<p><p>Machine Tool Vise market comprises of three main types - Single Station, Dual Station, and Multiple Station. Single Station vise is ideal for holding one workpiece at a time, providing precision and stability. Dual Station vise is designed to hold two workpieces simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity. Multiple Station vise can hold multiple workpieces at once, allowing for batch processing and reducing setup time. These different types cater to varying needs of manufacturers depending on the scale and complexity of their operations.</p></p>
