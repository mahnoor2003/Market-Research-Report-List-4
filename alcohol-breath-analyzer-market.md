<p><strong>The Alcohol Breath Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alcohol breath analyzers are classified into three main types: fuel cell, semiconductor, and infrared. Fuel cell analyzers are considered the most accurate and reliable, measuring alcohol levels by generating an electric current from a chemical reaction with ethanol. Semiconductor analyzers use a sensor that reacts with alcohol vapors, providing rapid results at a lower cost. Infrared analyzers measure alcohol levels by detecting the absorption of infrared light by ethanol molecules. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various settings such as law enforcement, workplace testing, and personal use.</p></p>
